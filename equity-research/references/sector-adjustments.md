# Sector Adjustments

Use this file when the default ROE/ROIC, leverage, free-cash-flow, and moat scorecard would mis-rank the business model. Apply `../../references/scoring-standard.md`: in every replacement scorecard, 3 means stronger or lower risk and 0 means weak, unavailable, or high risk.

## Financials

- Banks: replace ROIC with ROE relative to cost of equity, CET1/capital adequacy, NPL ratio, coverage ratio, NIM, deposit franchise, and credit-cycle exposure.
- Insurers: focus on embedded value, new business value, solvency ratio, investment yield, liability duration, product mix, and surrender risk.
- Brokers/exchanges: separate trading-cycle earnings from recurring asset-management, interest, data, and listing revenue.

## REITs And Property

- Replace net cash tests with loan-to-value, debt maturity ladder, interest coverage, secured debt ratio, and refinancing spread.
- Use FFO/AFFO, occupancy, rent reversion, cap rates, NAV discount, tenant concentration, and asset quality.
- For developers, emphasize presales, land bank quality, cash collection, impairment risk, and access to financing.

## Utilities And Infrastructure

- Use regulated asset base, allowed return, tariff reset cycle, utilization, concession life, capex needs, leverage, and dividend sustainability.
- Treat high payout as risky if maintenance capex, rate resets, or debt refinancing are underfunded.

## Cyclicals And Commodities

- Use mid-cycle margins, cost-curve position, balance-sheet survival, capacity cycle, inventory cycle, and replacement cost.
- Do not overvalue peak earnings or over-penalize trough earnings without normalizing the cycle.

## Platform Internet And Software

- Test revenue durability through users, retention, pricing power, take rate, ad load, merchant economics, cloud backlog, RPO, or net revenue retention.
- Adjust for SBC, buyback dilution, regulatory constraints, data/security rules, and capital allocation.

## Consumer

- Use volume versus price mix, channel inventory, distributor health, brand strength, repeat purchase, gross margin stability, and promotion intensity.
- For premium brands, check whether price increases are supported by volume and channel data.

## Manufacturing And Exporters

- Check FX, tariffs, overseas revenue quality, customer concentration, receivables, inventory, capacity expansion, and localization risk.
- Separate structural share gain from temporary restocking.

## Pre-Profit Biotech

- Score cash runway, trial stage, endpoint quality, regulatory path, partner validation, addressable market, and dilution risk.
- Do not use revenue multiples as the primary valuation method when revenue is not yet commercial.

## Output Discipline

When sector-specific replacements are used, state:

- Which default dimension was replaced.
- Why the replacement better fits the business model.
- Whether any red flag caps the rating.
- How the adjusted score affects the research label without becoming a mechanical buy/sell signal.

Use `Score Summary`, `Red Flags`, `Decision Impact`, and `What Would Change The View` in the final output.
