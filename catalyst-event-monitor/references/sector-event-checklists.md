# Sector Event Checklists

Use this file to adapt event monitoring to the user's industry. Keep the analysis event-driven; use `sector-industry-research` for a full industry cycle view.

## Technology Hardware And Semiconductors

- Earnings and guidance from leaders, suppliers, and major customers.
- Customer capex, order/backlog, inventory, utilization, pricing, product cycle, export controls.
- Watch: revenue guide, gross margin, capex commentary, lead times, customer concentration, supply bottlenecks.

## Software And Internet Platforms

- Earnings, product launches, pricing changes, regulation, take-rate changes, major customer wins/losses.
- Watch: growth durability, retention, ARPU/take rate, usage, ad pricing, cloud consumption, margin scale.

## Biotech And Pharma

- Clinical readouts, PDUFA/approval dates, advisory committee meetings, reimbursement, patent rulings, partnership data.
- Watch: endpoint quality, safety, trial powering, label scope, standard of care, cash runway, dilution risk.

## Banks And Financials

- Earnings, rate decisions, NIM, credit costs, capital returns, stress tests, regulatory changes.
- Watch: deposit beta, loan growth, asset quality, CET1/capital ratio, liquidity, funding cost.

## Consumer And Retail

- Earnings, same-store sales, channel checks, pricing actions, inventory, promotional intensity, product launches.
- Watch: volume versus price/mix, margin, traffic, inventory, consumer income sensitivity, brand health.

## Energy, Commodities, And Industrials

- Inventory reports, production cuts, capex plans, contract awards, spot/contract pricing, policy/tariff shifts.
- Watch: utilization, cost curve, spreads, backlog, replacement cost, capacity additions.

## Property, REITs, And Infrastructure

- Earnings, occupancy/rent updates, asset sales, refinancing, policy changes, rate moves.
- Watch: rent reversion, cap rates, debt maturity, occupancy, dividend coverage, funding cost.

## China / Hong Kong Policy-Sensitive Sectors

- Regulatory notices, policy meetings, subsidies, procurement, antitrust, data/security rules, Stock Connect changes.
- Watch: official wording, implementation details, eligible beneficiaries, funding source, margin cap, southbound/northbound flows.

## Lockup, Unlock, And Supply Events

- Lockup expiry, IPO share unlock, block trades, insider selling, index inclusion/exclusion, convertible issuance.
- Watch: float increase, holder cost basis, borrow cost, liquidity, demand support, management signal.
